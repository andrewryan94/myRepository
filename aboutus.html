About Us

Who we are

Welcome to EZ Shop, an online platform that links you with local farmers and delivers fresh, organic fruits directly to your door. Our team of fruit enthusiasts is passionate about providing you with healthy and delectable fruits. Let us share our love for fresh produce with you!

Why we do it

We hold the belief that fruits are beneficial not only for your physical well-being but also for your emotional happiness. Consuming fruits can strengthen your immune system, aid in digestion, and furnish your body with indispensable vitamins and minerals. Moreover, fruits have the potential to uplift your spirits, fulfill your sweet tooth, and spark your imagination.

How we started

In 2023, six friends started EZ Shop as a small project because we were tired of purchasing fruits from supermarkets. We desired to obtain fruits directly from farmers who value quality and the environment highly. We developed a website allowing individuals to order fruits online and deliver them to their residences.

How we grew

Our growth has been rapid since we launched, with thousands of customers across the country now using our service. We've expanded our team and network of farmers and constantly enhancing our platform by adding new features like ratings, reviews, recipes, and tips.

How we help

If you need a snack, dessert, gift, or a treat, EZ Shop can assist you in finding just what you need. Take your pick from a variety of fruits like apples, bananas, oranges, grapes, strawberries, and more. Additionally, you have the option to personalize your order by choosing your preferred size, quantity, and delivery date.
