Burger Bomb is a locally owned and operated restaurant located in the downtown area. Since our establishment just last year, we have already begun to take the local area by storm with our innovative recipes and top-notch ingredients. Burger Bomb's founders, Juan Tomas and his brother Jorge Tomas, are committed to providing you with a burger experience unlike any other. With a passion for freshness and creativity, we genuinely hope that you enjoy these out-of-this-world meals as much as we do.

Our business hours are Monday through Saturday from 11:00 AM until 11:00 PM. Call for information about our daily specials, featured Monday through Friday. Walk-in seating, reservations, and bar seating are all available to customers.

Our COVID-19 Safety Procedures

We have recently reopened for dine-in guests, and we are dedicated to protecting the safety and well-being of our customers and staff. In keeping with current CDC recommendations for public dining facilities, we routinely go above and beyond with our sanitation and precautionary procedures. Our current COVID-19 protocol includes all of the following:

  1. Hand sanitizer is given to all guests upon arrival
  2. Guests must wear masks prior to being seated
  3. Tables are spaced over 6 feet apart to remain compliant with social distancing
  4. Limiting the number of guests inside the restaurant at one time
  5. Menu is available online via a scannable QR code
  6. Regular cleaning and sanitation throughout the restaurant, to include tables, chairs, bathrooms, and waiting area
  7. Tables are thoroughly sanitized after each use
  8. Optional outdoor seating is available
