Brothers Juan and Jorge Tomas have always shared a passion for trying new food. Over the years, they have sought out the best restaurants all over the nation. So, when Juan pitched the idea of opening a game-changing burger joint, both brothers were overjoyed to bring their unique style and recipes to the local area. Established just last year, Burger Bomb has already been a major hit, with ample praise in online reviews and on social media platforms.
